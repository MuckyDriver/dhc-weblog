History
Roblox Development History

My past in the world of game development and website development is limited. It was only 4 years ago or around that when I started designing my first Roblox game. Even though I did make games on Roblox in 2017 they were made without any scripting, no original models and mainly small as I had no experience on how to make Roblox games back then. In 2019 I found myself interested in learning the language to create games on Roblox which is Luau. The exact game I was working on is unknown as I have changed the original files with something else. During Mid 2018 I created my first fully created game NukeLab which is a game all about nuking a small island and see the destruction of the buildings etc. Early 2019; I developed a game called "Ultimate Sad Su Tart Story", which was created during a trend. To this day this game is the most successful games that I have created.

Late 2019 marked the year I started the longest Roblox project - named Mucky Driver! This game is all about players driving vehicles around a map and collecting points to upgrade to a new vehicle. The original name for this game was to be "Offroader Sim 19" however at the time Roblox moderation decided that was not allowed and that is how I thought of "Mucky Driver" instead. Mucky Driver includes a vast map, ten plus vehicles and challenges you can do. However, early 2023 I decided the project is taking too long and the inspiration behind the game was thin so the project was discontinued. The Mucky Driver project has given me new skills and knowledge on how to create a Roblox game and has provided me experience on what to do and not what to do when developing a Roblox game.
