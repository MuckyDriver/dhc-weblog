Website History

The DHCWeblogistics website has had many changes over the years, this is mainly due to me knowing more and more about website development programming. Take a look throughout history of this website and watch as it transforms through time.

Original DHCWeblogistics

The first ever version of DHCWeblogistics, created with little knowledge of web design.

DHCWeblogistics 2021

After 1 year of learning web design, this website was made in attempt to get commissions.

DHCWeblogistics 2022 v1.0

A new edition to 2021's site, a revisit to the same purpose of getting commissionable work on making websites.

DHCWeblogistics 2022 v2.0

An updated layout and style to the previous version.

DHCWeblogistics 2023

First version of DHCWeblogistics to have purpose of being a portfolio website.
